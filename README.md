## Queries

### Query 1 – Customers Who Made Purchases

What are the last names and emails of all customers who made purchases in the store?

### Query 2 – Albums and Artists

What are the names of each album and the artist who created it?

### Query 3 – Customers Per State

What are the total number of unique customers for each state, ordered alphabetically by state?

### Query 4 – States with Many Customers

Which states have more than 10 unique customers?

### Query 5 – Artists with "Symphony" Albums

What are the names of the artists who made an album containing the substring "symphony" in the album title?

### Query 6 – Artists in Specific Playlists

What are the names of all artists who performed MPEG (video or audio) tracks in either the "Brazilian Music" or the "Grunge" playlists?

### Query 7 – Artists with Many MPEG Tracks

How many artists published at least 10 MPEG tracks?

### Query 8 – Long Playlists

What is the total length of each playlist in hours? List the playlist id and name of only those playlists that are longer than 2 hours, along with the length in hours rounded to two decimals.

### Query 9 – Creative Query: Longest Tracks Per Genre

Define a new meaningful query using at least three tables and a window function.

**What this query achieves:**
This query identifies the five longest tracks within each music genre.
